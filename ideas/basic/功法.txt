功法depends on 灵根 type and cultivation realm. Different 灵根 types will have different affinities for certain 功法, affecting the effectiveness and efficiency of cultivation. 
As the player progresses through the cultivation realms, they will unlock new 功法 that are more powerful and complex. there would be three type of 功法：单灵根功法，属性功法，无属性功法。
where 单灵根功法 can only be learned by players with a specific 灵根 type, 属性功法 are功法 that have elemental attributes that correspond to the player's 灵根, 
and 无属性功法 are neutral功法 that can be learned by any 灵根 type but may be less effective than those tailored to specific 灵根 types.

功法获益效果：
- 获取灵根xp
- 获得特殊技能
- 获得特殊状态
- 增加属性点

功法招式：
  单一招式：需找齐所有残卷，炼制功法。
  正常招式：每一个残卷都能炼制出一个招式，集齐所有残卷能炼制出完整功法。以及额外获得特殊招式或效果。
